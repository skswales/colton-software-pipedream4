Z88 Transfer

The Z88 filing system originally supplied with PipeDream 4 to enable PipeDream documents to be transferred back and forth to the Z88 is no longer supported on 32-bit RISC OS computers. An archived copy of the last release of !Z88 may be downloaded from the web site for use with 26-bit RISC OS computers.

The Z88 lives on at rakewell.com - please do visit their site. Also see the Z88 Wiki.
