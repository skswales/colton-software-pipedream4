Installation

Installing PipeDream on your computer is now very simple. You can freely download the latest version of PipeDream from the website.

PipeDream is supplied in a ZIP format archive, which is readable by SparkFS on your RISC OS computer. Simply double-click on the PipeDream archive file to open it. You can copy the !PipeDream application directory from the archive to the desired location on your disc or network server.

You will probably find it helpful to download a copy of the PipeDream examples archive from the website at this point, and extract its contents to wherever you wish.
