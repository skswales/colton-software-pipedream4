About PipeDream 4

What is PipeDream 4?

PipeDream 4 offers all the facilities you would expect from the following separate programs:

  • word processor;
  • spreadsheet;
  • database;
  • charts package.

There are other programs, usually called integrated packages, which also offer these facilities. Often, such packages provide a rather limited range of features and their appeal lies mainly in being cheaper than buying four separate packages. If you use such programs, you quickly find that your software is not powerful enough for your requirements.

Buying PipeDream 4 was certainly cheaper than buying four separate packages and now, thanks to Richard Colton, it is entirely free-to-use. PipeDream 4 was designed to offer not only the range of features found in typical integrated packages, but also the degree of power you expect from state-of-the-art individual packages. As you get to know PipeDream 4, you will find that it contains many facilities in each of the four categories which do not exist in any other package.

What Can PipeDream 4 Do?

PipeDream 4 has a unique and powerful design. This is based around the concept of a cell. Cells can contain a line, part of a line, text, numbers or formulae — which can be highly complex calculations. If a cell contains a formula, the result of calculating the formula is displayed in the cell.

PipeDream 4’s unique design enables you to perform your word processing, spreadsheet and database work within the same window. Suppose you have prepared a financial or scientific report and want to include tables of calculations within it. With other packages, trying to mix text and live calculations can cause difficulties. You would need to make copies of your tables from your spreadsheet or database and ‘paste’ them into your report in your word processor. Typically, after you had done this, the data in the tables would change, and you would have to repeat the operation. Not so with PipeDream 4; simply create the tables within the text - no cutting and pasting is necessary because the tables remain ‘live’ and can be updated at any time.

You can use PipeDream 4 for word processing: create letters, reports, invitations, film scripts and books.

You can use PipeDream 4 as a spreadsheet: create ledgers, simple shopping lists, complex scientific models and cash flow forecasts.

You can use PipeDream 4 as a database for storing parts lists, names and addresses, details of personal collections and mark sheets.

You can also do things with PipeDream 4 that would be very difficult or impossible with other programs.

For example:

  • Include live calculations in your word processing.
  • Annotate your spreadsheets with formattable paragraphs of text.
  • Write a letter in your word processor and send it to a selection of people from your names and addresses database.
  • Check the spelling of your spreadsheet.
  • Sort into alphabetical order the list that you have just typed into your word processor.
  • Create a new field containing calculations in your database and sort on it.
  • Create live charts from data in your spreadsheets and databases and include the charts in your reports.

As you will see, with PipeDream 4 you can carry out all these operations easily.
